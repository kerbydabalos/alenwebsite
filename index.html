About Paloma's Beach Resort

  • Entrance Fee: ₱50 per head. Free if renting a cottage.
  • Cottage: ₱300(Weekdays)- ₱500(Weekends) 15 persons per cottage
  • Overnight Stay: ₱800 per cottage (6:00 PM - 6:30 AM)
  • Parking Fee: Free
  • Opening Time: 7:00 AM
  • Closing Time: 5:30 PM
  • Lifeguard on Duty: 7:00 AM – 5:30 PM
  • Visitors: Mixed(Local & Foreign)
  • Most Visiting Months: December
  • Owner: Marianette Marcaban

Safety & Rules

  • Watch your children at all times.
  • Swim within designated areas only.
  • Always follow the warning signs.
  • Never swim after consuming alcohol.
  • Always keep your trash in the trashcan.
